# Hotel Revenue Strategy Dashboard – Milestone 4

## Project Overview

This dashboard is part of the **HotelRevAI – AI Driven Revenue Analysis for Hotels** project.

Milestone 4 focuses on transforming historical booking and revenue data into **actionable revenue strategy insights** using **Power BI**. The goal is to support pricing decisions, identify high-performing segments, and uncover upsell opportunities.

---

## Revenue Strategy Dashboard

The Revenue Strategy Dashboard provides a consolidated business view of hotel performance, pricing effectiveness, and revenue optimization indicators.

![Revenue Strategy Dashboard](screenshots/Revenue_Strategy_dashboard.png)

---

## Business Objectives

The dashboard is designed to help hotel management:

- Analyze revenue and occupancy behavior across seasons
- Evaluate room category performance
- Assess pricing effectiveness using ADR and RevPAR
- Identify high-revenue booking channels
- Detect revenue improvement opportunities

---

##  Key KPIs Implemented

The dashboard highlights critical revenue management metrics:

- **Revenue** – Total earnings from bookings  
- **Occupancy %** – Utilization of available rooms  
- **ADR (Average Daily Rate)** – Revenue per booking  
- **RevPAR (Revenue per Available Room)** – Pricing + Occupancy efficiency  

These KPIs enable quick executive-level performance evaluation.

---

## Revenue Strategy Insights

### Revenue & Seasonal Performance

Seasonal analysis helps identify demand fluctuations:

- Peak seasons generate higher revenue potential
- Low seasons indicate promotion opportunities
- Shoulder seasons require balanced pricing strategy

---

### Room Category Performance

Room categories (Economy, Luxury, Premium) are analyzed to determine:

- Revenue contribution per category
- Booking concentration patterns
- High-value room segments

---

### Pricing Effectiveness (ADR & RevPAR)

ADR and RevPAR metrics evaluate pricing strategy effectiveness:

- High ADR → Strong pricing power
- High RevPAR → Optimal pricing + occupancy balance

---

### Booking Channel Analysis

Revenue comparison across booking channels:

- Direct bookings vs OTA performance
- Channel profitability evaluation
- Strategic channel prioritization

---

## Pricing Strategy Logic

A season-driven pricing approach is applied:

- **Peak Season** → Premium Pricing Strategy  
- **Shoulder Season** → Standard Pricing Strategy  
- **Low Season** → Discount / Promotion Strategy  

This aligns room pricing with demand behavior to maximize revenue.
