# Hotel Business – Understand & Improve Revenue (Milestone 2)

## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing hotel booking data to understand revenue performance, occupancy trends, booking behavior, and customer segmentation.  
The dashboard is built using **Power BI** and extends **Milestone 1** with advanced KPIs, time-based analysis, and guest insights.

---

## Objectives
- Analyze hotel revenue and occupancy performance
- Identify booking trends across time (daily, seasonal, yearly)
- Compare **Direct vs OTA** booking channels
- Classify and segment guests based on booking behavior
- Identify high-value customers for business insights

---

## Tools & Technologies
- **Power BI**
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)**
- **Power Query**
- **GitHub** (Version Control & Documentation)

---

## KPI measures
Total Revenue, Occupancy %, ADR, and RevPAR are implemented using DAX and are used across multiple visuals instead of standalone KPI cards to maintain a compact and insight-focused dashboard layout.

---

## Analysis Performed

### ⏱ Time-Based Booking Analysis
- Daily booking trends
- Seasonal booking trends
- Yearly booking overview

### Booking Channel Analysis
- Revenue comparison: **Direct vs OTA**
- Booking volume by channel
- ADR comparison across booking channels

### Guest Analysis & Segmentation
- Guests by country
- Guests by booking source
- Guests by length of stay (Short / Medium / Long)
- Customer segmentation:
  - Guests Type
  - High spenders

## Key Insights
- Winter season shows the highest booking demand
- Direct bookings generate higher revenue efficiency
- OTA channels contribute significantly to booking volume
- Loyal guests and high spenders contribute a major share of total revenue

---

## Conclusion
This dashboard provides actionable insights into hotel performance and customer behavior, helping management make data-driven decisions related to pricing, marketing, and customer retention.
